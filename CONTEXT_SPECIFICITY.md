# Context Specificity

## Domain Intelligence

This system encodes cricket reasoning:

* Powerplay vs death strategy
* Spin vs pace matchups
* Left-right batting combinations

---

## Example Functions

* compute_pressure_stability()
* get_opposition_vulnerability()
* recommend_fielding_changes()
* generate_captain_brief()

---

## Context Factors

* Pitch (spin/pace)
* Weather (dew, humidity)
* Phase (powerplay, middle, death)

---

## Why It Matters

Cricket decisions are:

* situational
* dynamic
* context-heavy

This is NOT generic analytics.
